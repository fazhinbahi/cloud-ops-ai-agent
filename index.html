About Us

Every cloud team faces the same silent problem: the environment moves faster than the people watching it. Security gaps quietly open. Costs compound unnoticed. Incidents brew for hours before anyone gets paged. And the engineers who should be building — spend their days firefighting instead.

CloudAgent was built to close that gap. We took the knowledge, instincts, and workflows of the best cloud and DevOps engineers we know — and encoded them into an autonomous AI agent that runs continuously, acts intelligently, and never burns out.

This isn't another monitoring dashboard that adds to your alert fatigue. CloudAgent doesn't just observe your environment — it understands it. It knows the difference between a temporary CPU spike and a runaway process. Between a firewall rule that needs attention and one that will cost you a headline. Between a cost anomaly worth flagging and one worth fixing right now.

Our mission is simple: every engineering team — regardless of size — should have access to the kind of cloud operations expertise that was previously only available to the largest organisations in the world. CloudAgent makes that possible.

Solutions

Everything your cloud needs, handled autonomously

Five interconnected capabilities that work together to give your cloud environment a continuous layer of intelligence.

🔍
Intelligent Scanning

Complete Cloud Health Intelligence

Five specialised AI agents deploy simultaneously across your GCP environment — auditing security posture, infrastructure health, cost efficiency, live incident signals, and deployment stability — all in under 3 minutes. Every finding is ranked by severity, blast radius, and business impact so your team always knows what to address first.

Security Audit IAM Review Infra Health Cost Analysis
🧠
Autonomous Proposals

Not Recommendations. Executable Fixes.

For every finding, CloudAgent doesn't just flag it — it writes the fix. Each proposal includes the exact GCP API call, the expected outcome, a blast radius rating, and full rollback instructions. Your team reviews and approves with full context, or lets the agent act automatically based on your policy rules.

Blast Radius Rating Rollback Ready One-Click Approve
🤖
Policy-Driven Execution

Safe Automation, Human Override

Low-risk, reversible fixes execute automatically — no ticket, no delay, no manual effort. High-risk or irreversible actions always surface to a human with a one-click interface. Every action, whether auto-executed or manually approved, is logged with outcome, timestamp, and rollback state. Full audit trail. Zero surprises.

Auto-Execute Human Override Full Audit Log
💸
FinOps Intelligence

Find What Your Cloud is Quietly Wasting

Cloud waste doesn't announce itself — it compounds quietly across idle nodes, overprovisioned VMs, missed committed use discounts, and dev environments running in production at 3am. The FinOps engine cross-references 30 days of utilisation data, calculates exact annual impact per resource, and acts on what's safe to fix immediately. One client recovered $94,000/year in the first scan.

Root Cause Analysis

The Investigation That Finishes Before You Join the Bridge

The moment an incident is detected, the RCA agent activates. In 90 seconds it pulls 6 hours of error logs across all affected services, traces the first failure forward through dependencies, cross-references deployment history and infrastructure changes, and generates a structured report — Timeline, Root Cause, Blast Radius, Recommended Fix — before a human touches anything. Mean time to resolution drops. On-call burnout drops harder.
